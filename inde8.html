The Tislit Gorges, A piece of Magic

To get there :
From Taliouine take the N10 east (Tazenakht road) for about 30km.Then on the left on the Assaïs road, travel 30 km at the end of thepaved road (new). At the end of the village 2 tracks, the one on the left leads to the attic of the cliff of Tisguit. (To see absolutely!) Take the one on the right. 3 kms to do in 4X4 but easy to do on foot (about 1 hour) There, you arrive on a small gorge of the Oued walking between boulders of Cyclopean rocks, in colorful shrubs and a few isolated palm trees. A small wonder !
