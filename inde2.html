Bin EL Ouidane, An Exceptional National Heritage

Azilal - Located in the heart of the province of Azilal between Wadi El Abid and Assif Ahansal and having as a symbol its famous crystal blue waters, Lake Bin El Ouidane is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful landscapes in Morocco, offering a nature very rich in colors, splendid views and a unique biodiversity. A true wonder of nature, the lake nestled between the Atlas mountains is renowned for its setting conducive to relaxation, serenity and rejuvenation of body and mind. Perched at an altitude of over 800 meters, with its immense body of water covering nearly 4,000 hectares, Lake Bin El Ouidane is considered the highest pond in Africa. Combining splendid natural landscapes and biological diversity, the lake is, in fact, endowed with abundant agricultural and forest resources and considerable fish resources as well as hundreds of species of fauna and flora.

A dream setting, an extremely rare beauty and a paradisiacal landscape are among others the predominant characteristics of this jewel of the Kingdom which abounds in a strong population of fish such as pike, black bass, carp and pike perch making it the ideal spot and the campsite par excellence for those who love pond or river fishing. Obviously, Bin El Ouidane offers itself as a paradise for all water sports lovers. Its calm and limpid waters make it the destination par excellence for fans of boat rides and kayaking, as well as lovers of thrills and extreme sports such as jet skiing and water skiing.

Bin El Ouidane also derives its originality from the fact that this lake is one of the most visited and popular sites in recent years by foreign tourists and Moroccan visitors, thanks to significant efforts made by the public authorities for the opening up of this area to facilitate access and encourage tourists to go there. In an interview, the provincial tourism delegate in Azilal, Mustapha Ichou, stressed to MAP that this marvelous, breathtaking lake has experienced significant tourism promotion efforts, the fruits and fervor of which are already showing. known worldwide.
